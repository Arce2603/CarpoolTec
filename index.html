About Us

About

This application will help ITESM students to connect with others who are willing to do car-sharing. The website will facilitate to look for people who drives or lives nearby on similar hours, so the user might ride with him/her.

Vision

Create a safe and suitable carpooling service for students.

Objective

By enhancing the carpooling services we intend to reduce the carbon footprint caused by the car’s emission of CO2, also this gives the students a cheaper way to get to the school , without the need of a payment subscription.

How to start?

  1. Click on the

  2. Fill out the information using your itesm mail
